# FX Broker, Prop Firm & Trading Education — Career Page Reference

A manual-check list of 100+ companies relevant to forex traders, educators,
and coaches looking for new roles. Most do **not** use Greenhouse/Ashby/Lever,
so CareerOps can't auto-scan them. Treat this as a bookmark list — open a
broker's careers page every week or two, grab any role that fits, paste it
into CareerOps's **New** evaluation page.

The subset that *does* work with CareerOps's auto-scanner is already seeded
(see `src/lib/portals.ts`) — Coinbase, Kraken, Robinhood, Interactive
Brokers, Gemini, Stripe, Wise, etc.

## How to use this list

1. Bookmark this file. Browsers let you `Ctrl+F` to find a company fast.
2. Once a week, open 5–10 of these and look for new postings.
3. When something fits (educator, trading coach, content, analyst, community,
   onboarding, account management), copy the role's title and full JD.
4. Paste into CareerOps **New** → get a Claude evaluation and tailored CV.

For the auto-scan list (Coinbase, Kraken, Robinhood, Stripe, Wise, etc.),
you don't need to do anything manual — just keep the **worker** terminal
running and a nightly scan fills the database.

## Adding more brokers

If you find an FX broker that *does* use Greenhouse/Ashby/Lever, add it
through the **Scan** page in the app — no code change needed. The quickest
way to check: try `https://boards.greenhouse.io/{company}` in your
browser. If that page loads their jobs, the slug works.
